GENERAL INFO ON
COVID-19


COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the
most recently discovered coronavirus.This new
virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak
began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.
COVID-19 is now a pandemic affecting many
countries globally.
